Mayhem Heroes

Mayhem Heroes is a Third Person 4 player Splitscreen Coop Shooter set in a Candy themed world.
It was created by 19 Students over the course of a single semester.
The Players try to defeat the evil-turned Candy King and his minions by using their special abilities.

The game was developed in the Unreal Engine 4.

My Project Roles

My role was that of the Lead Engineer. I was responsible for almost all technical matters, such as setting up git repositories and access for the whole team, configuring PCs with the necessary Software or just providing support.
I decided on using the Unreal Engine 4 for this project because of my prior experience and also because of the engines great set of tools.
One of my responsibilities also was the organization of all programmers over the course of the project, of which there were 6 total. This includes creating and assigning suitable tasks for each of my team members, weekly status meetings and support in technical matters.
Additionally I also created several gameplay systems in the game, namely the base systems for the player characters and abilities, basic abilities for the Healer and Scientist classes, as well as the "Black Hole" for the Scientist.
I also created several environmental obstacles, such as cotton candy bushes and a cola river.
