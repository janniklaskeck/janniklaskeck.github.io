What the Cluck

What the Cluck is a 4vs4 Splitscreen cross-platform multiplayer game.
The two teams try to capture the enemies barns and farm by throwing their own chickens in their vicinity.
To achieve their goals they can tackle their opponents or pass chicken between each other.

The game was developed in the Unreal Engine 4 and works cross-platform between the PC and PS4.

My Project Roles

As the Lead Engineer on the What the Cluck project, I was responsible for almost all technical tasks.
I setup the project from the software side and managed the engineering department, consisting of 12 programmers total.
My tasks also included creating and assigning tasks regarding all game features to my department, support and/or coaching for all team members and creating important game features.
My main contributions consist of almost all of the underlying base code for most gameplay systems as well as overhauling/fixing several of the key gameplay systems during the development.
